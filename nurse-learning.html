Nurse Learning Platform

An educational simulation for nursing students.

Project Overview

The Nurse Learning Platform is a serious-game prototype designed to support nursing education through scenario-based simulation in a controlled virtual environment. The project focuses on modeling patient-care routines, clinical decision-making, and procedural compliance aligned with nursing protocols. The platform allows students to practice interactions with patients, medical equipment, and the surrounding environment while receiving structured feedback based on their actions and choices.

As the Gameplay Programmer, I was responsible for designing and implementing the core gameplay systems that drive player interaction and scenario progression. This included both technical architecture and hands-on content implementation.

Details & Responsibilities

  • Designed and implemented an interaction system enabling actions on environmental objects, medical equipment, and patient avatars.
  • Developed a Scenario Manager using a finite state machine, responsible for controlling scenario flow, validating player actions, and supporting both linear and branching clinical outcomes based on protocol adherence.
  • Implemented custom UI components to present feedback, instructional prompts, and interaction states clearly to the player.
  • Created and integrated visual assets, including environment and interaction-related elements, ensuring consistency between gameplay logic and visual representation.
  • Collaborated iteratively on gameplay tuning and presentation, balancing educational clarity with interactive responsiveness.
